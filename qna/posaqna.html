QUESTION: IF A QUESTION IS NOT ON HERE?
RESPONSE: PLEASE REFER THE CONSUMER TO THE CORRESPONDING "800" NUMBER: 866-434-3079.

QUESTION: WHAT IS THE POLICE OFFICERS SUPPORT ASSOCIATION?
RESPONSE: THE POLICE OFFICERS SUPPORT ASSOCIATION IS A PROJECT OF A FEDERAL POLITICAL COMMITTEE CALLED LAW ENFORCEMENT FOR A SAFER AMERICA. LAW ENFORCEMENT FOR A SAFER AMERICA PUSHES TO GET CANDIDATES ELECTED WHO SUPPORT EMERGENCY RESPONDERS AND WILL PUSH TO GET LEGISLATION AND POLICY PASSED THAT PROMOTES THE SAFETY OF OUR EMERGENCY RESPONDERS.

QUESTION: ARE YOU A MEMBER OF THE ORGANIZATION?
RESPONSE: NO, I WORK FOR A COMPANY HIRED TO CONDUCT THESE CALLS FOR THE ASSOCIATION.

QUESTION: IS THIS A DEMOCRAT OR REPUBLICAN ORGANIZATION?
RESPONSE: THIS ORGANIZATION IS NOT DEMOCRATIC OR REPUBLICAN; IT'S A BIPARTISAN ORGANIZATION FOCUSING ON THE NEEDS OF LAW ENFORCEMENT OFFICERS.

QUESTION: WHAT IF I ASK A QUESTION YOU DO NOT KNOW?
RESPONSE: YOU KNOW THAT'S A REALLY GOOD QUESTION, AND I DON’T WANT TO GIVE YOU AN INCORRECT RESPONSE. SO INSTEAD, IF YOU WOULD LIKE MORE INFORMATION REGARDING THE DRIVE, I CAN GIVE YOU A NUMBER TO CALL: 866-434-3079.

QUESTION: WHAT IS THE TAX STATUS OF THIS ORGANIZATION?
RESPONSE: THE POLICE OFFICERS SUPPORT ASSOCIATION IS A 527 ORGANIZATION UNDER THE IRS TAX CODE. DUE TO ITS PARTICULAR TAX STATUS, CONTRIBUTIONS ARE NOT TAX DEDUCTIBLE.

QUESTION: WHERE IS THE ORGANIZATION LOCATED?
RESPONSE: THE POLITICAL COMMITTEE IS LOCATED IN SARASOTA, FL.

QUESTION: DO THEY HAVE A WEBSITE?
RESPONSE: YOU CAN VISIT THE POLICE OFFICERS SUPPORT ASSOCIATION ON THE WEB AT www.policeofficerssupportassociation.org

QUESTION: HOW MUCH GOES TO THE POSA?
RESPONSE: THERE IS NO SET PERCENTAGE. THERE ARE SUBSTANTIAL FUNDRAISING EXPENSES, PROCESSING FEES, AND NORMAL FEC COMPLIANCE EXPENSES. BUT THE PLAN IS TO HAVE ENOUGH FUNDS LEFT OVER TO SUPPORT THE CANDIDATES WHO WILL TRULY HELP THE CAUSE.

QUESTION: DOES THIS HELP OUT LOCALLY?
RESPONSE: THIS IS A NATIONAL PROGRAM.

QUESTION: WHO DO YOU WORK FOR?
RESPONSE: I WORK FOR A PROFESSIONAL FUNDRAISING COMPANY HIRED BY THE PAC.
