QUESTION: WHAT IS THE POLICE OFFICERS ALLIANCE PAC
RESPONSE: TTHE POLICE OFFICERS ALLIANCE PAC IS A POLITICAL COMMITTEE THAT PUSHES TO GET CANDIDATES ELECTED WHO SUPPORT OUR NATION’S LAW ENFORCEMENT OFFICER AND HELP ELECT CANDIDATES THAT WILL GIVE THE PROPER ATTENTION TO THE WIDESPREAD ISSUES IN LAW ENFORCEMENT.

QUESTION: ARE YOU A MEMBER OF THE ORGANIZATION?
RESPONSE: NO I WORK A COMPANY HIRED TO CONDUCT THESE CALLS FOR THE ASSOCIATION.

QUESTION: IS THIS A DEMOCRAT OR REPUBLICAN ORGANIZATION?
RESPONSE: THIS ORGANIZATION IS NOT DEMOCRATIC OR REPUBLICAN; IT'S A BIPARTISAN ORGANIZATION FOCUSING ON THE NEEDS OF LAW ENFORCEMENT OFFICERS.

QUESTION:WHO IS THE HEAD OF THIS ORGANIZATION?
RESPONSE: THIS POLITICAL COMMITTEE WAS ORGANIZED AND IS MANAGED BY A GROUP OF CITIZENS CONCERNED WITH THE DETERIORATING SUPPORT FOR EMERGENCY RESPONDERS
