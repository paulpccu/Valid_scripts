QUESTION: WHAT IS THE POLICE ADVOCACY COMMITTEE PAC?
RESPONSE: THE POLICE OFFICERS ACTION COMMITTEE PAC IS AN INITIATIVE OF POSC PAC AND IS A POLITICAL COMMITTEE DEDICATED TO ENCOURAGING AMERICANS TO SUPPORT CANDIDATES WHO RECOGNIZE THE CHALLENGES FACING OUR NATION'S LAW ENFORCEMENT OFFICERS AND WHO WILL WORK TOWARD STRONGER PUBLIC SAFETY POLICIES, IMPROVED RESOURCES, BETTER TRAINING, AND INCREASED ATTENTION TO THE CRITICAL ISSUES AFFECTING LAW ENFORCEMENT AND COMMUNITY SAFETY.

QUESTION: ARE YOU A MEMBER OF THE POLICE ADVOCACY COMMITTEE PAC?
RESPONSE: NO, I WORK FOR A PROFESSIONAL FUNDRAISING COMPANY WHICH HAS BEEN CONTRACTED BY THE POLICE ADVOCACY COMMITTEE PAC TO HELP.

QUESTION: IS THIS A DEMOCRAT OR REPUBLICAN ORGANIZATION?
RESPONSE: THIS ORGANIZATION IS NOT DEMOCRATIC OR REPUBLICAN; IT IS A NON PARTISAN POLITICAL ORGANIZATION FOCUSING ON THE NEEDS OF OUR VETERANS.

QUESTION: WHO IS THE HEAD OF THIS ORGANIZATION?
RESPONSE: THIS POLITICAL ORGANIZATION WAS ORGANIZED AND IS MANAGED BY A GROUP OF CITIZENS CONCERNED WITH THE NUMBER OF VETERANS IN CONGRESS.

QUESTION: WHAT IS THE TAX STATUS OF THIS ORGANIZATION?
RESPONSE: CONTRIBUTIONS AND DONATIONS TO THE POLICE ADVOCACY COMMITTEE PAC ARE NOT CHARITABLE CONTRIBUTIONS AND ARE NOT DEDUCTIBLE AS CHARITABLE CONTRIBUTIONS FOR STATE AND FEDERAL INCOME TAX PURPOSES.

QUESTION: WHERE IS THE POLICE ADVOCACY COMMITTEE PAC?
RESPONSE: THE POLICE ADVOCACY COMMITTEE PAC IS HEADQUARTERED IN CHICAGO, IL.

QUESTION: HOW MUCH GOES TO THE POLICE ADVOCACY COMMITTEE PAC?
RESPONSE: THERE IS NO SET PERCENTAGE. THERE ARE SUBSTANTIAL FUNDRAISING EXPENSES, PROCESSING FEES, AND NORMAL FEC COMPLIANCE EXPENSES. BUT THE PLAN IS TO HAVE ENOUGH FUNDS LEFT OVER TO SUPPORT THE CANDIDATES WHO WILL TRULY HELP THE CAUSE.
