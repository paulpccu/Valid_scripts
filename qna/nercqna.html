QUESTION: IF A QUESTION IS NOT ON HERE?
RESPONSE: PLEASE REFER THE CONSUMER TO THE CORRESPONDING "800" NUMBER: 866-439-6480.

WHAT IS THE NATIONAL EMERGENCY RESPONDERS COALITION?
RESPONSE: THE NATIONAL EMERGENCY RESPONDERS COALITION IS A PROJECT OF A FEDERAL POLITICAL COMMITTEE CALLED LAW ENFORCEMENT FOR A SAFER AMERICA PAC. LAW ENFORCEMENT FOR A SAFER AMERICA PAC PUSHES TO GET CANDIDATES ELECTED WHO SUPPORT EMERGENCY RESPONDERS AND WILL PUSH TO GET LEGISLATION AND POLICY PASSED THAT PROMOTES THE SAFEY OF OUR EMERGENCY RESPONDERS.

QUESTION: ARE YOU A MEMBER OF THE ORGANIZATION?
RESPONSE: NO, I WORK FOR A COMPANY HIRED TO CONDUCT THESE CALLS FOR THE PAC.

QUESTION: IS THIS A DEMOCRAT OR REPUBLICAN ORGANIZATION?
RESPONSE: THIS ORGANIZATION IS NOT DEMOCRATIC OR REPUBLICAN; IT'S A BIPARTISAN ORGANIZATION FOCUSING ON THE NEEDS OF EMERGENCY RESPONDERS OFFICERS.

QUESTION: WHO IS THE HEAD OF THIS ORGANIZATION?
RESPONSE: THIS COMMITTEE WAS ORGANIZED AND MANAGED BY A GROUP OF CITIZENS CONCERNED FOR THE DETERIORATING SUPPORT FOR EMERGENCY RESPONDERS

QUESTION: WHAT IS THE TAX STATUS OF THIS ORGANIZATION?
RESPONSE: LAW ENFORCEMENT FOR A SAFER AMERICA PAC IS A 527 POLITICAL ORGANIZATION UNDER THE IRS TAX CODE. DUE TO ITS PARTICULAR TAX STATUS CONTRIBUTIONS ARE NOT TAX DEDUCTIBLE.

QUESTION: WHERE IS THE ORGANIZATION LOCATED?
RESPONSE: THE POLITICAL COMMITTEE IS LOCATED IN SARASOTA, FL

QUESTION: DO THEY HAVE A WEBSITE?
RESPONSE: YOU CAN VISIT THE NATIONAL EMERGENCY RESPONDERS COALITION ON THE WEB AT www.nationalemergencyresponderscoalition.org

QUESTION: HOW MUCH GOES TO THE NERC?
RESPONSE: THERE IS NO SET PERCENTAGE. THERE ARE SUBSTANTIAL FUNDRAISING EXPENSES, PROCESSING FEES, AND NORMAL FEC COMPLIANCE EXPENSES. BUT THE PLAN IS TO HAVE ENOUGH FUNDS LEFT OVER TO SUPPORT THE CANDIDATES WHO WILL TRULY HELP THE CAUSE.

QUESTION: DOES THIS HELP OUT LOCALLY?
RESPONSE: THIS IS A NATIONAL PROGRAM.
